Data Accessibility
problem in somalia

accessing the basic  information is extremely difficult in somalia when it comes to the various branches of the government which means that somalis are partially deprived of their rights.

somalia is a developing country and is developing in many areas such as education, agricultural production as well as technology but it is still difficult to get the basic information you are entitled to from various government agencies and that is a challenge for somali society because The government is responsible for providing the public with basic information and the people are not aware that their rights to the government have not yet been received and this makes it normal for you to see people who do not understand the true meaning of basic information for Somali citizens .

With this in mind, we are launching a major campaign against this problem by the  government agencies and we establish  a data center in Somalia called (Somalia national information center) to ensure that all Somali citizens everywhere in the world have same access to the basic information they need such as financial and tax information ,so the citizens can feel confident in their government and put an end to all corruptions in government institutions
